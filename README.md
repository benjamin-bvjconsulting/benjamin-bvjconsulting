# Benjamin Jaboeuf

Legal ops advisor and builder in San Francisco. Former IP litigator and legal ops in several startups, now helping pre-seed to Series A founders get fundraise-ready, and building the AI-native version of that practice.

### What I do
I run BVJ Consulting, a legal ops practice covering cap table management, SAFE structuring, corporate hygiene, data room prep, and fundraising readiness for early-stage startups. I work as an embedded, fractional advisor, and increasingly I turn that advisory work into software.

### What I'm building
- **ReadyRoom** ([readyroom.vc](https://readyroom.vc)): an ambient data room that keeps a startup investor-ready from day one, instead of scrambling at diligence time.
- **Founder toolkit**: open, interactive tools on [bvjconsulting.com](https://bvjconsulting.com), including a SAFE calculator, a valuation cap guide, and a co-founder equity split calculator.
- **AI-native legal ops tooling**: internal systems built on the Anthropic API that replace per-client spreadsheets with structured, queryable data rooms.

### How I work
Next.js, TypeScript, Vercel, and the Anthropic API. I recently built a multi-agent Data Room Reconstructor at the Cerebral Valley / Google I/O hackathon. I think in terms of one problem, one feature, one stream of revenue.

### Background
Transatlantic by design: I serve both US and French-speaking founders raising in the US. Fifteen years of IP litigation in France, ten-plus years inside US startups, and BVJ Consulting since 2023.
